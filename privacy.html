Privacy Policy

Your privacy is our priority. YOSIDI CAD does not collect identifying user data and does not upload your plans to external servers.

Data We Do Not Collect

We do not collect identifying data such as your name, email address, phone number, location, contacts, photos, drawings, or files. Your plans and settings are stored locally on your device and are not uploaded to our servers.

Interaction and Analytics Data

The app may collect limited app interaction data, purchase history, and basic technical data through Firebase/Google Analytics. This data is used for analytics, product improvement, and app functionality. It is not used for advertising, is not sold, is not shared with data brokers, and is not used for tracking.

No Unnecessary Permissions

The app does not access your location, contacts, microphone, camera, photos, or any other personal data. It does not require you to create an account, provide an email address, or log in. You can use YOSIDI CAD completely anonymously.

Data Storage

All your drawings and settings are stored locally on your iOS device using the app's local file system. This data remains on your device and only you have access to it. If you uninstall the app, all stored data will be automatically deleted.

Secure Payments

All in-app purchases (DXF/PDF exports) are processed securely by Apple through the App Store. We do not store, process, or have access to any payment information such as credit card numbers or banking details. All payment information is managed exclusively by Apple.

Bluetooth Laser Meters

If you use the Bluetooth laser measure connection feature, the app will request permission to access your device's Bluetooth. This connection is used solely to receive measurements from compatible laser devices. No personal data is transmitted via Bluetooth, and the connection is local between your iPad/iPhone and the laser measure.

Website

Our website (www.yosidi.com) uses Cloudflare Web Analytics to measure aggregate traffic without cookies, without fingerprinting, and without storing personally identifiable data. It may store your language preference locally in your browser; this information is not sent to third parties.

Children's Privacy

YOSIDI CAD is not specifically directed to children. The app does not require an account, email address, or identifying data, and we do not knowingly request personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy occasionally. Changes will be posted on this page. We recommend reviewing this policy periodically.

Contact
