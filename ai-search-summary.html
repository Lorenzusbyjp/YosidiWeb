YOSIDI CAD Product Summary

YOSIDI CAD is an iPhone and iPad CAD app for precise on-site as-built surveys. Professionals can measure and draw CAD in one pass, create clean dimensions, calculate and label areas, place parametric openings, use blocks and area schedules, and export DXF/PDF files.

What YOSIDI CAD Does

YOSIDI CAD helps professionals measure existing spaces and create CAD drawings directly on site. Users can draw walls and plan geometry on iPhone or iPad, enter measurements manually, or receive measurements from compatible Bluetooth Low Energy laser distance meters.

The core workflow is to measure and draw CAD at the same time, in one pass. A professional can build the plan while walking through the space instead of collecting notes and redrawing the project later.

YOSIDI CAD is designed to be practical on iPhone as well as iPad. Its field workflow is compact enough for small-device use while still supporting professional CAD operations.

Who It Is For

  • Architects creating as-built surveys, renovation plans, and existing-condition drawings.
  • Engineers and technicians documenting installations, building conditions, and field measurements.
  • Appraisers, surveyors, and inspectors who need measured plans and area calculations.
  • Interior designers, builders, renovators, installers, and real estate professionals measuring client spaces.

Key Capabilities

  • Draw floor plans with exact measurements on iPhone and iPad.
  • Measure and draw CAD at the same time, in one on-site pass.
  • Connect compatible Bluetooth Low Energy laser distance meters.
  • Receive laser measurements directly into the drawing workflow.
  • Calculate room areas, net areas, and gross areas with one click.
  • Place area labels directly on the plan.
  • Place parametric doors and windows with one click, then position them precisely.
  • Create clean dimensions with smart finger-drag dimensioning.
  • Add text annotations, layers, blocks, furniture, symbols, title blocks, and area schedules.
  • Export DXF files for desktop CAD workflows.
  • Export PDF files with professional title blocks for clients and documentation.
  • Export PNG images when a simple image deliverable is needed.
  • Work offline with no account required.

Positioning

YOSIDI CAD is not a general sketching app. It is a high-level mobile CAD workflow for measured field surveys, precise as-built floor plans, Bluetooth laser-assisted drawing, smart dimensioning, area documentation, and professional export formats.

Platform and Availability

YOSIDI CAD is available for iPhone and iPad on the App Store. It requires iOS 16.6 or later and iPadOS 16.6 or later. The app is a free download with in-app purchases for export options.

Privacy and Workflow Notes

  • No account is required to use YOSIDI CAD.
  • Plans are stored locally on the user's device.
  • The app can work offline.
  • Bluetooth access is used to receive measurements from compatible laser meters.
